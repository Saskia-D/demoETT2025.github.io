## Emerging Topics in Typology 2025

Following the success of the 2021 workshop series and the 2024 conference, Emerging Topics in Typology (ETT) returns for a third edition. ETT 2025 will be a 3-day conference from June 3rd to 5th, 2025, organized by and for PhD students in diversity linguistics.
